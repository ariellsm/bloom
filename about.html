About Bloom

We take healthcare personally.

More than a clinic, we're on a mission to flip the healthcare script. We reject the rushed, impersonal approach—because you deserve to be seen, heard, and cared for as a whole person.

Dr. Naomi Mostkoff DNP, APRN, FNP-BC
Your Provider

Meet Naomi

Dr. Naomi Mostkoff provides thoughtful, evidence-based care and partners with patients to support both immediate needs and long-term health goals. Her work emphasizes inclusive, affirming primary care with a focus on endocrine, metabolic, and hormonal conditions, reproductive and sexual health, mental health, postpartum wellness, and care for the queer community.

Clinical Experience & Focus

  • Primary care and women's health
  • Endocrinology, including complex diabetes management
  • Thyroid disease, bone health, and medical weight management
  • LGBTQ-affirming care & Queer Health
  • Post-surgical and post-transplant care

Education

  • BA in Anthropology, University of Florida (2009)
  • BSN, University of Florida (2013)
  • DNP, Family Nurse Practitioner, University of Florida (2019)

Malissa Badal-Yang MSN, APRN, FNP-C
Your Provider

Meet Malissa

Malissa Badal-Yang focuses on building strong, trusting relationships and provides patient-centered, whole-person care grounded in listening, education, and partnership. She values evidence-based care and has particular interests in women's health, geriatric care, and addressing common dermatologic concerns as part of comprehensive primary care.

Clinical Experience & Focus

  • Management of acute and chronic conditions
  • Diabetes, hypertension, and respiratory disease
  • Mental health conditions and chronic pain
  • Preventive care and minor procedures
  • LGBTQ-affirming primary care

Education

  • AS in Nursing, LaGuardia Community College (2006)
  • BSN, Western Governors University (2019)
  • MSN, Family Nurse Practitioner, University of Texas at Arlington (2025)
